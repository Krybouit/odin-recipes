Guinness Cupcakes with Guinness Frosting

Description

Delicious cupcakes that will melt in your mouth

Ingredients

  • 1 (12 fluid ounce) can or bottle Irish stout beer
  • ½ cup milk
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 3 large eggs
  • ¾ cup sour cream
  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • ¾ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking soda

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease two 12-cup muffin tins or line cups with paper liners.

  2. Whisk stout beer, milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract together in a bowl until
    evenly blended. Beat in eggs 1 at a time, beating well after each addition. Mix in
    sour cream. Whisk together flour, sugar, cocoa powder, and baking soda in a
    separate bowl. Gradually mix the flour mixture ingredients into the beer mixture.

  3. Spoon cupcake batter into the prepared muffin cups, filling each 3/4 full.

  4. Bake in the preheated oven until cupcakes are risen and set in the middle but still soft and tender, about 25 minutes.
    Cool in the tins, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely, about 1 hour.
